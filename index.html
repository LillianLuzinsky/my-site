About

12 years ago, I left my demanding job and ventured into freelance design in order to regain my creative energy and do more rewarding work with the community. I recently undertook the challenge of learning to code and successfully completed the Software Engineering immersive course at General Assembly. Today, I consider myself a coder who appreciates design. I have an interest in learning how to build products that are user focused and I would like to keep on learning to be a better problem solver. I thrive working in a team where I can contribute creatively everyday in my new career as a developer.
